    Frequently Ask Question

    What are the ingredients of our Halo-halo?

    Our Halo-halo consist of shaved ice with Creamy evaporated milk, Sweetened banana, Sweetened corn, Nata de coco, Agar agar, Jelatin,, Sweetened beans, Leche flan, and Ube halaya.

    Where can I find Gloria's Halo-halo?

    Gloria's Halo-halo is located at 319 Herbosa St, Tondo, Manila.

    What are the most popular items at Gloria's Halo-halo?

    The most popular items at Gloria's Halo-halo are the Halo-halo dessert, Leche Flan (caramel custard) and Ube Halaya.

    How can I contact Gloria's Halo-halo?

    You can contact Gloria's Halo-halo in our Facebook page.

    Do you offer non-dairy milk?

    Unfortunately we don't offer non-dairy products.

    What brand of milk you use in your Halo-halo and Mais Con Yelo?

    The milk that we use in our Halo-halo and Mais Con Yelo is "Alaska Evaporada Classic"
